# Radix Guild — Getting Started

## Join in 3 Minutes

### Step 1: Join the Telegram Group
Ask for an invite link or find us in the Radix community channels.

### Step 2: Register Your Wallet
In the TG group, send:
```
/register account_rdx1your_address_here
```
Your Radix wallet address links to your Telegram account.

### Step 3: Mint Your Badge
Go to https://radixguild.com/mint and click "Join the Guild".
Connect your Radix Wallet and approve the transaction.
Your Guild member badge appears in your wallet.

### Step 4: Start Participating
Back in the TG group:
- `/proposals` — see what's being voted on
- Tap vote buttons on active proposals
- `/propose Your idea here` — create your own proposal
- `/badge` — check your tier and XP

## Badge Tiers

| Tier | XP | What You Can Do |
|------|-----|----------------|
| Member | 0+ | Vote on proposals |
| Contributor | 100+ | Claim bounties, submit proposals |
| Builder | 500+ | Create tasks, mentor |
| Steward | 2,000+ | Verify work, treasury proposals |
| Elder | 10,000+ | Run for council |

## Proposal Types

### Simple Decision
```
/propose Should the Guild adopt 72-hour voting as default?
```
Creates For / Against / Amend buttons. 72 hour vote.

### Multiple Choice
```
/poll Which DAO platform? | CrumbsUp | Consultation v2 | TG-native | Other
```
Up to 6 options. Top choice wins.

### Temperature Check
```
/temp Is there interest in running a validator node?
```
24 hour, non-binding. Quick pulse on an idea.

### Refine a Proposal
```
/amend 1 Updated version of the proposal with changes
```
Links back to the original. Full history tracked.
